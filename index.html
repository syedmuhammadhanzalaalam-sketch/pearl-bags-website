Our Artisan Story

At LUMINA, every bag begins as a vision and ends as a wearable piece of art. Our artisans spend hours meticulously hand-threading each pearl, ensuring unmatched quality and durability.

We source sustainable, high-luster pearls to minimize our environmental footprint while maximizing the elegance of our finished products. This is slow fashion at its most beautiful.
